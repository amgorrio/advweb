Important People

Original Members of the Stamdford Town School Committee
  • William T. Godfrey--Chairman
  • Albert H. Emery--Secretary
  • Warren F. Cressey
  • Walter R. Dolan
  • Walter A. Greeney
  • Ellery G. Peckham
  • Florence M. Shipley

These people were part of the Town School Committee, they prescribed rules for the management of the schools, for the studies, classifications, discipline, transportation, length of school day, term and years. They also prescribe books subject to the approval of the State Board of Education, keep all school property in repair, and approve or disapprove all plans for new school buildings.
